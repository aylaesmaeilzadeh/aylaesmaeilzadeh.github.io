Ayla Esmaeilzadeh

Computational Biologist | M.Sc. in Biotechnology

I am passionate about understanding biological systems at the molecular level through computational approaches. My background is in biotechnology and cell and molecular biology, and my research focuses on molecular dynamics simulations and structural bioinformatics to investigate biomolecular interactions, drug delivery systems, and the structure and function of proteins in the context of therapeutic development and biomolecular design.

I am particularly interested in developing computational strategies for disease-relevant challenges in cancer, neurodegenerative diseases, and women's health. My long-term research goal is to integrate molecular modeling with machine learning and generative AI to design next-generation proteins, peptides, and other therapeutic biomolecules.

A Few Things About Me

💜 Favorite Color

🍲 Favorite Foods

Traditional Iranian Ghormeh Sabzi holds a special place in my heart, and I can never say no to a good plate of pasta.

🎵 Music

📚 Books

My bookshelves are usually filled with mystery and crime novels, although I also enjoy reading self-help books that offer new perspectives on personal growth.

🎬 Movies

🧘 Wellness

Yoga and meditation are my favorite ways to recharge, stay grounded, and maintain balance.

❄️ Favorite Season

✨ A Personal Note

I enjoy moving between seemingly different worlds, from molecular modeling and computational biology to poetry, music, baking, and stories. For me, science and creativity are not separate pursuits but complementary ways of understanding and experiencing the world.
